🕉️ Monday: Rudra Puja and Fasting ▼

🌅 1. Early Morning Ritual ▼

  • Wake up early (ideally before sunrise)
  • Take a full bath (head bath if possible)
  • Wear clean, preferably white or light blue clothes
  • Clean your prayer space or altar

🛕 2. Prepare Your Rudra Puja ▼

  • Picture or Shivling (if available)
  • Clean water (Ganga Jal if possible) or milk
  • Bilva (Bel) leaves
  • A diya (lamp), incense
  • Some flowers (white or blue preferred)
  • A fruit or small offering

🙏 3. Puja Steps (Simple Rudra Worship) ▼

  • Light the diya and incense
  • Sprinkle water on yourself (purification)
  • Offer water/milk to Shivling or image (Abhishekam)
  • Offer Bilva leaves and flowers while chanting
  • Chant "Om Rudraya Namah" 108 times
  • Sit quietly in meditation for 5-10 minutes
  • Close with prayer: "O Rudra, remover of darkness..."

🌱 4. Fasting Guidelines ▼

  • Observe full or partial fast
  • Avoid non-veg, alcohol, smoking
  • Maintain calm demeanor
  • Perform acts of charity if possible
